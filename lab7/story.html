Adopting My Cat Beans

I never had a cat before I got Beans but I really wanted a pet and a dog seemed like it would be too difficult to convince my landlord. There is a no pet policy, so I figured a cat would be an easier ask.

My landlord said it would be ok as long as they don't destroy the apartment (a very reasonable request). I was so happy. I started looking for cats that afternoon and started putting in applications. The Liberty Humane Society reached out to me and said they have the perfect Cat for me.

I got on a zoom call and saw Beans running around having a good time and decided that he was the one. I picked him up a few days later. He is a really good Cat, he does tricks, he cuddles, likes everyone, and doesn't get the zoomies at 2 in the morning.
